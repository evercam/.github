## 📌 What is Evercam?

Evercam is a powerful visual monitoring and site analytics platform tailored for the construction industry. It unifies real-time video, time-lapse, drone imagery, 360° capture, and BIM (Building Information Modeling) overlays into a single interface to provide end-to-end project visibility. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

With Evercam, construction teams can:

- Monitor progress remotely via live streams and securely shared views. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- Generate scheduled time-lapses and review historical footage. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  
- Overlay BIM / 4D models onto real site imagery to validate “plan vs actual.” :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
- Compare images over time, annotate, measure, and show “x-ray” views for deeper insights. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  
- Monitor gate vehicle traffic (via ANPR), integrate drone surveys, and generate reports. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  
- Integrate with major construction and enterprise software stacks (e.g. BIM tools, project management, ERP) through APIs and connectors. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}  

Evercam is trusted across **1,000+ projects in 20+ countries** with tens of thousands of users and supports projects collectively valued over **$400B+**. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

## 🚀 Why Use Evercam?

- **Better Project Oversight** — You don’t need to visit the site physically; real-time visibility replaces many in-person checks. :contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}  
- **Reduce Rework & Mistakes** — By cross-checking against plans and catching deviations early, teams can avoid costly rework. :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}  
- **Communicate with Stakeholders** — Share live views, progress photos, time-lapses, and annotated images with clients, investors, or remote teams. :contentReference[oaicite:19]{index=19}  
- **Document Evidence** — In disputes, change orders, or claims, you have a timestamped visual record of site conditions.  
- **Seamless Workflow Integration** — Works alongside your existing tools (BIM, PM, ERP) rather than in a silo.
